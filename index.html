03How the AI phone agent gets built

Four steps, about two weeks.

Most agents are live within 10 business days. The long pole is you listening to it, not us building it.

Discovery call

30 minutes. We listen to your current voicemail, your top 20 FAQs, your booking flow, and what "good" sounds like to you.

Day 1

Agent built

We train the agent on your services, prices, hours, and handoff rules. You get a private number to call and test.

Day 2–5

You break it

You and one trusted staffer try every weird question you've ever been asked. We tune until it stops fumbling.

Day 5–8

Live & monitored

We port forward your main line (or add it alongside), and spend the first week watching transcripts with you daily.

04Is this a fit?

We're small on purpose. Here's who we work best with.

Good fit

  • 1–25 person service businesses where a missed call is a missed job.
  • Owner-operators who'd rather call or text than file a ticket.
  • Trades, wellness studios, salons, pet services, property managers, cleaning companies.
  • Anyone already paying a call-answering service and getting generic receptionists.
  • Within our on-site corridor: Toronto → Hamilton along the QEW.

Probably not a fit

  • Enterprise IT with a compliance checklist — we're not SOC-2 audited.
  • Businesses that need 24/7 follow-the-sun coverage from humans.
  • Anyone outside the Toronto–Hamilton corridor for on-site work (remote-only, fine).
  • "We just need a cheap VA" — an AI agent isn't cheaper than a VA on day one; it's cheaper by month six and never sleeps.
