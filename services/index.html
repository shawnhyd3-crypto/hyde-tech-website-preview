07Before you book

Straight answers to the stuff you're probably wondering.

Do you do contracts?

Month-to-month on managed services (you can cancel with 30 days' notice). One-off work is quoted flat before we start. No annual lock-ins.

Are you insured?

Yes — $2M commercial general liability. Certificate available on request.

Do you do residential?

Some — cameras, WiFi, smart-home installs. We don't do residential remote IT support as a retainer. For that we'll point you at Geek Squad or a neighbour's teenager.

What if my site is outside your service area?

Remote support, no problem. On-site, we'll quote travel honestly — if the drive doesn't make sense for the job, we'll say so and refer you out.

Can the AI phone agent replace my receptionist?

It can handle 60–90% of what a receptionist does — answering, screening, booking, confirming. For relationship-heavy customer calls, you still want a human. Most clients run it as an always-on backup rather than a replacement, and let it catch after-hours and overflow.
