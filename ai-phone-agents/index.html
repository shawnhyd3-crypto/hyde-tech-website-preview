03What it does

Everything a good receptionist does. Faster.

Core

Answers on the first ring

24/7. Never puts callers on hold. Never sends them to voicemail.

Booking

Writes to your calendar

Google Calendar, Jobber, Calendly, or native API. The agent knows your real availability and books real slots.

Handoff

Texts you the summary

Every call you'd have handled yourself shows up as a short text with the caller's number, what they wanted, and what was booked.

Intelligence

Knows your pricing

Quotes from your actual price list. Explains what's included. Flags out-of-scope jobs for you to quote manually.

Escalation

Hands off when it matters

Emergencies, angry customers, anything off-script — the agent gets your cell on the line or sends an urgent text.

Ownership

Your line, your script

Keep your number, your voice profile, your vocabulary. The agent sounds like your business — not a generic bot.
